It pulled me back to school. My M.S. in Data Analytics Engineering at Northeastern (GPA: 3.95) gave me the other side of the stack building the pipelines, not just auditing them.

Now I am looking for a team where I can put it all together clean data models, reliable pipelines, and dashboards that turn raw data into decisions people can act on.
